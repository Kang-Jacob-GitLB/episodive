Screen Structure

The home screen uses a BottomSheetScaffold with a collapsible bottom sheet (mini player). The main content is a vertical scroll hosting 9 distinct sections of horizontal and vertical lists.

MVI Architecture

HomeState

  • Loading
  • Success(playingEpisodes, userRecentPodcasts, randomEpisodes, userTrendingPodcasts, followedPodcasts, localTrendingPodcasts, foreignTrendingPodcasts, liveEpisodes, channels)
  • Error(message: String)

HomeAction

  • PlayEpisode(episode: Episode)
  • ResumeEpisode(playedEpisode: Episode)
  • ToggleLikedEpisode(episode: Episode)
  • ClickPodcast(podcastId: Long)
  • ClickChannel(channelId: Long)

HomeEffect

  • NavigateToPodcast(podcastId: Long)
  • NavigateToChannel(channelId: Long)

The Home screen is one of the 4 bottom navigation destinations. Clicking a podcast navigates to Podcast Detail; clicking a channel navigates to Channel Detail. Playing/resuming episodes triggers the player via use cases without navigation.
