Screen Structure

The onboarding screen uses a HorizontalPager with 4 pages to guide the user through initial setup. A PagerIndicator and navigation buttons appear at the bottom of every page.

Pager Pages

Page 1 — Welcome

Displays the Episodive logo and a greeting message. A single "Get Started" button advances to the next page.

Page 2 — Category Selection

A LazyVerticalGrid (3 columns) of CategoryButton chips. Users must select at least 3 categories. A DraggableScrollbar appears on the right edge. Attempting to proceed with fewer than 3 selections triggers the ToastMoreCategories effect.

Page 3 — Podcast Selection

A LazyColumn with paginated PodcastSimpleItem rows, populated by GetUserRecommendedPodcastsPagingUseCase (max 50). Users can follow/unfollow podcasts matching their selected categories.

Page 4 — Completion

A checkmark animation with a congratulatory message. After a 3-second delay, SetFirstLaunchOffUseCase runs. A "Start" button navigates to the home screen.

The onboarding screen is shown only when UserPreferences.isFirstLaunch is true. After completion, SetFirstLaunchOffUseCase sets the flag to false and the user is routed to the Home screen.
