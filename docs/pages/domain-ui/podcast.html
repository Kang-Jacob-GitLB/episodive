PodcastsSection

Horizontal scrollable podcast list with a section title header. Composes a SectionHeader with a LazyRow of PodcastItem elements. Uses snap fling behavior for smooth page-like scrolling with 16dp spacing and horizontal content padding.

PodcastItem

Square image card with title and episode count. The image uses a 1:1 aspect ratio with extraLarge shape (28dp corners). Title is truncated to 2 lines. Width is fixed at 140dp.

PodcastDetailItem

Full detail card for the podcast detail screen. Uses a horizontal Row layout with a 96dp image on the left and content on the right. Content includes the title (1 line), a follow toggle button, a FlowRow with owner/date/count icon-text pairs, and an HTML description (4 lines).

PodcastSimpleItem
